How it works

Built for real days,
not ideal ones.

Chronic illness doesn't follow a clean schedule. Health Flare is designed for the full range — the good days, the hard ones, and everything in between.

Track what matters

Log symptoms, energy, sleep, and mood in a few taps. Add notes when you want to — skip them when you don't. No required fields, no friction.

Spot your patterns

Health Flare surfaces symptom cycles, sleep correlations, and treatment effects — connections that are impossible to spot when you're in the middle of it.

Share on your terms

Create a shareable summary for your doctor or care team. You choose exactly what's included, how much detail to show, and when to revoke it.

Built for families too

Caring for a child with a complex condition? Health Flare supports multiple profiles with the same privacy guarantees for every person in your care.

Privacy by design

No cloud. No accounts.
No compromises.

Your health data is some of the most sensitive information that exists. We built Health Flare so it never has to leave your device.

  • Stored on-device, encrypted at rest Protected by your device's Secure Enclave (iOS) or StrongBox (Android) — hardware-level security.
  • No accounts required No sign-up, no profile, no identity tied to your data. Open the app and it's ready.
  • Zero analytics or telemetry No crash reporters that phone home. No advertising identifiers. No tracking of any kind.
  • Sharing is always optional When you share with a care provider, you control what's shared, in what form, and you can revoke it at any time.
